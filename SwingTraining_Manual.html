Overview

What is SwingTraining?

⚡

AUTO Detection

7-layer algorithm detects every swing automatically — no button press during practice. Covers golf clubs (3I–PW), aluminum bat, wood bat, and tennis racket.

📐

Tempo Analysis

Measures backswing vs. downswing timing separately. Evaluates each swing against sport-specific targets with four quality grades: EX / GD / FA / PO.

📊

Garmin Connect FIT

Sessions save as standard Garmin FIT activities with 16 custom data fields — session summaries and per-swing charts sync to Garmin Connect automatically.

🔄

Adaptive Threshold

Detection sensitivity auto-calibrates from your last 10 swings (min. 5). Adapts to each person's individual swing strength. Manual reset in Settings.

📋

4-Page Session Stats

After each session: Summary overview, Tempo trend graph, Quality distribution chart, and Swing timing (ABDIF) analysis. Navigate with UP / DOWN.

🤲

Left & Right Handed

Full support for both orientations via the on-watch Settings menu. Club selection and handedness persist automatically across sessions.

SwingTraining runs entirely on-watch — no phone is needed during a session. Data syncs to Garmin Connect the next time your watch connects to the app.
User Manual

How To Use

Modes
🤖

AUTO Mode

Press START and swing freely. The app detects every swing automatically. Club is not tracked — all swings score against Golf targets. Toggle with UP or DOWN in IDLE.

🎯

MANUAL Mode

Press START → select club → swing. Records which club was used per swing. Supports all 11 clubs and sports (3I–PW, AL-BAT, WD-BAT, TENNIS) with sport-specific scoring.

Swing Metrics

What Gets Measured

Quality Scale
EXCELLENT
EX · Score 3 · ≤ 30% of tolerance
Both backswing and downswing within ±30% of sport target. Best achievable result per swing.
GOOD
GD · Score 2 · ≤ 60% of tolerance
Solid swing within ±60% of target. Minor deviation from ideal — still a strong performance.
FAIR
FA · Score 1 · within tolerance
Detectable variance. Within the overall tolerance window but not optimal for that sport.
POOR
PO · Score 0 · outside tolerance
Timing breakdown outside the tolerance range. Focus area for targeted practice.
Scoring rule: Each swing receives two independent scores — one for backswing timing, one for downswing timing. The final quality is the minimum of the two, so both phases must be on-target for a high grade.

Detection Engine

7-Layer Algorithm

L1
Duration Gate
300–1500 ms (Golf)
Rejects events too short (noise) or too long (sustained arm movement). Each sport has its own min/max detection window.
L2
Peak Accel Gate
≥ 3.0 G required
Confirms a genuine swing versus incidental movement. Adaptive threshold recalibrates from your rolling 10-swing history.
L3
Backswing Ratio Gate
≥ 25% of duration
Validates a proper backswing phase — distinguishes a full swing from a punch or half-strike motion.
L4
Sample Rate
25 Hz · 40 ms tick
Fixed polling rate ensures consistent timing resolution across all 19 supported Garmin devices.
L5
Smoothness Filter
SensorPipeline MA
Moving-average smoothing on raw accelerometer data suppresses high-frequency vibration artifacts from grip contact.
L6
Energy Analysis
SwingFeature extraction
Locates the backswing / downswing boundary via zero-crossing of the acceleration envelope, extracting precise BS/DS split timing.
L7
Adaptive Threshold
Last 10 swings · min 5
Continuously recalibrates detection sensitivity from your rolling history. Reset via CALIBRATION in Settings when switching users.
Cooldown period after each detection prevents double-counting the follow-through phase — Golf: 2000 ms  ·  AL-BAT: 1500 ms  ·  WD-BAT: 1500 ms  ·  Tennis: 1000 ms.
